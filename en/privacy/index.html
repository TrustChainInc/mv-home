Policy for Use and Protection of Personal Information

Trustchain Inc. (the “Company”) considers your personal information careful and complies with the laws and regulations in relation to personal information.
The Company complies with the Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection (hereinafter referred to as the “Information and Communications Network Act”) and the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (the “Personal Information Protection Act”).

01. Items of personal information to be collected

The Company collects the following personal information through the customer inquiry page and e-mail for corporate inquiries:
· Required fields
    -Name, email
· Optional fields
    -Mobile phone number
· The following information may be generated and collected in the course of service use or business processing.
    -Service usage record, access log, cookie, access IP information

02. Purpose of processing personal information

The Company uses the collected personal information for the following purposes:
· Identification according to customer and company inquiries, responses and complaints handling after identification

03. Personal information collection method

The Company collects personal information that you enter, and automatically generated information (cookie, IP) on your computer.

04. Period of processing and retention period of personal information

After the purpose of collecting and using personal information is achieved, the relevant information shall be destroyed without delay.
However, if it is necessary to retain it under the provisions of the relevant laws and regulations, the Company shall keep customer information for a certain period prescribed by the relevant laws and regulations as follows:
· Retention items: Personal information and counseling details entered at the time of receipt of inquiries
· Grounds for retention: Act on Consumer Protection in Electronic Commerce, Etc. (Records on Consumer Complaints or Dispute Handling)
· Retention period: 1 year
In addition, personal information that must be kept for a certain period in accordance with laws and regulations and applicable laws are as follows:

Retention items Applicable Law Retention Period
Records on contract or subscription withdrawal Act on Consumer Protection in the Electronic Commerce Transactions, Etc. 5 years
Records on payment and supply of goods, etc. 5 years
Records on consumer complaints or dispute resolution 3 years
Records on display/advertisement 6 months
Books and supporting documents for all transactions stipulated by the tax laws Framework Act on National Taxes 5 years
Records of electronic financial transactions Electronic Financial Transactions Act 5 years
History of service visits Protection of Communications Secrets Act 3 months

05. Personal information destruction procedure and method

After the purpose of using the collected personal information is achieved, the Company destroys the information without delay according to the period of use and retention. The destruction procedure and method are as follows:
· Destruction procedure
   -Personal information entered by you for service use, etc. is transferred to a separate database (separate filing cabinet in the case of paper) after the purpose of use, such as
    service termination, is achieved, and is deleted or destroyed after the lapse of the retention period for any of the reasons for protection of information according to internal
    policies and other relevant laws (refer to the period of retention and use of personal information as seen above).
   - Personal information transferred to separate database will not be used for any purpose other than being retained unless it is required by law.
· Destruction method: Personal information printed on paper is destroyed by crushing or incineration with a shredder, and personal information stored in electronic file format is deleted using a technical method that cannot reproduce the record.

06. Provision of personal information to third parties

In principle, the Company does not provide personal information to the outside except with the customer's consent or in accordance with relevant laws and
regulations. However, exceptions are made in the following cases:
· In case of obtaining separate consent from the information subject;
· By the provisions of laws and regulations, or when there is a request from the investigation agency in accordance with the procedures and methods stipulated in the laws for the purpose of investigation; or
· If providing in a form that cannot identify a specific individual for statistical preparation, academic research, or market research

07. Consignment of processing of personal information

The Company does not consign the processing of personal information.

08. Rights of users and their legal representatives and how to exercise them

1) The information subject may request to view, modify, delete, and withdraw consent from registered personal information at any time.
2) If the information subject requests to suspend the processing of personal information, the Company suspends all or part of the processing according to the request without delay.
3) For children under the age of 14, a legal representative may exercise each of the above rights.
4) The Company handles the personal information canceled or deleted at the request of the information subject or legal representative as specified in the personal information processing policy and does not view or use it for any other purpose.
5) Each of the above rights can be exercised by the information subject by contacting the personal information management division of the Company (Refer to Section 10) in writing, by phone or e-mail, and may be restricted in accordance with the provisions of the Personal Information Protection Act and other laws.

09. Installation, operation and rejection of automatic personal information collection devices

The Company operates 'cookies' that store and find your information from time to time. Cookies are very small text files sent to your browser by the server used to operate the Company's website and are stored on your computer's hard disk.
· Purpose of use of cookies, etc.: Analyze users' page access frequency and visit time
  You have the option to install cookies. Therefore, you may set options in the web browser to
  accept all cookies, check each time a cookie is saved, or refuse to save all cookies.
· Example of setting method (in case of Internet Explorer): Tools at the top of the web browser > Internet Options > Personal Information
  However, if you refuse to install cookies, there may be difficulties in providing services.
