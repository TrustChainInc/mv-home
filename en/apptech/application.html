Convenient App

MileVerse provides a smartphone application service. Customers can easily exchange each company's mileage for MVP, MileVerse's own points, through the MileVerse app and use it for payment. MileVerse app provides major functions such as mileage exchange function for each company mileage, payment function using barcode or QR code, digital gift certificate purchase function, gift giving function, and double security function using a pin code.

Main page

It is the first screen of the MileVerse app. The most important component, the menu, is placed in the center, and the screen can be scrolled left and right to enhance readability and convenience for customers. In addition, centered on the menu bar, an advertisement area at the top and an event area at the bottom are arranged to facilitate delivery of major information.

It is a menu that allows you to exchange points of each company for MVP or obtain MVP through purchase

Pay

It is a menu that supports the barcode payment function at offline affiliate stores with the obtained MVP. If you touch the desired brand icon, a barcode is created at the top.

Shopping

It is a menu that allows you to purchase mobile gifticons of affiliated brands with the obtained MVP

Gift

It is a menu that allows you to purchase and present mobile gifticons to your friends with the obtained MVP, or to present the MVP directly

Reward

It is a reward mall established through partnership. It consists of “Shopping Reward”, where you can receive rewards by purchasing products, and “Direct Reward”, where you can receive rewards by watching advertisements.

My Wallet

This is a menu where you can check the virtual assets you have, including MVP, and exchange between MVP and MVC, which is MileVerse's virtual currency.
