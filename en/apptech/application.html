Convenient App

The MileVerse App provides key functions and various entertainment activities, such as exchanging each company's mileage for MVP, on-site payment using barcodes, purchasing digital gift certificates, and giving gifts, and supports double security using Pin Code. - MileVerse App 3.0 was released in December 2022 to further enhance user convenience, and the UI was renewed in March 2024.

App Guide

Main page

As the first screen of the MileVerse app, it onsists of functions such as news of Mileverse, my information, and movement of major ontents, so that users can enjoy various contents of MileVerse more conveniently.

My MileVerse

This is a page where you can check the status of your MVP, current rating information, MVP usage history, etc.

Earn MVP

This is a menu where you can earn MileVerse-Points in various ways.

Shopping

It is a menu composed of various shopping contents such as offline payment and digital gift certificate purchase with the acquired MVP.

Things to do

You can enjoy a variety of content, including real-time news, roulette, and donations.

My Wallet

This is a menu where customers can check the digital assets they have, including MVPs.

Withdrawal service

It is a menu that exchanges corporate points with MVP and withdraws after registering an account.
