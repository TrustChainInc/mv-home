MILEVERSE

Mileverse creates a new paradigm for easy payment with mileage.

GREETING

Hello.

My name is William Jung, CEO of Mileverse.

MileVerse is a compound of Mileage + uniVerse, and it is an app service created by TRUSTCHAIN Inc. to create a new ecosystem of mileage.

According to data from Statistics Korea, while the domestic mileage market is growing at more than 20% every year, consumers have been passive in the use of mileage except few consumers due to a lot of restrictions in applications and use of such mileage and points such as the cases that companies that issue points such as franchisees and coffee houses prevent consumers from using their mileage only inside.

We launched the Mileverse app service with the idea of solving these problems and making it easy for consumers to use the mileage of various companies that they have and use like cash.

Through this, our goal is to create MileVerse’s unique mileage economy by creating a mileage ecosystem that can produce mutual cooperation with consumers, corporations and franchisees and establishing a “new paradigm for mileage payment”.

We ask for your interest and love while watching the new mileage ecosystem that Mileverse is creating.
Thank you.

William Jung, CEO, MileVerse
