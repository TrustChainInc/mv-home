Architecture

Application Layer

As a general smartphone app, this area allows users to utilize the service as they input and output data.

Service Layer

This area allows utilization of individual modules used in the app, and each module is operated by user action.

Infra Layer

Made up of hardware and software systems to provide the MileVerse platform service environment,
this is where the information of MileVerse platform is stored, protected and monitored.

BlockChain Layer

The blockchain area operates in the Ethereum mainnet
platform environment to utilize the aforementioned ERC-20 tokens.

Smart Contract Layer

This area creates smart contracts of Ethereum where ERC-20 tokens are
generated through smart contracts. This area includes codes that exchange MVP for MVC.

Token Ecosystem

MileVerse platform builds a dual token ecosystem to address the risks associated with fluctuations in token values.

MVC [MileVerse-Coin]

Issued as Ethereum-based ERC-20 tokens, asset tokens with asset value.
Issued in a limited amount of 3 billion; exchange for MVP (payment token) available.

MVP [MileVerse-Point]

Issued in the database, tokens used for mileage exchange and payment.
There is no limit on the amount of issuance; 1 MVP is worth KRW 1.
