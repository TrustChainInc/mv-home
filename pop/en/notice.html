Revision Item 3. To add the provisions to “Article 4-2 Notice of Sources of Personal Information Collected from Any Subject Other than Data Subjects”

Revised content: Adding an item

  • Additional content:

    Article 4-2 (Notice of Sources of Personal Information Collected from Any Subject Other than Data Subjects)

    When a user provides personal information of a third party other than the user himself or herself, the Company notifies the user of the right to demand the source of collected personal information and the purpose of processing personal information only at the request of a third party as data subject of the personal information in accordance with Article 37 of the Personal Information Protection Act, and the right to request the suspension of processing personal information in accordance with Article 37 thereof by using one of text message, social media message, and email. In this case, if there is no separate request of the third party as data subject, the Company will not inform the third party of the aforementioned matters separately.
